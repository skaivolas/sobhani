1-7[edit]

001:001 In the name of God, the Almighty, the Merciful. Bismi Allahi alrrahmani alrraheemi
001:002 Praise be to God, the Lord of the worlds. Alhamdu lillahi rabbi alAAalameena
001:003 The Almighty, the Merciful. Alrrahmani alrraheemi
001:004 Sovereign of the Day of Judgment. Maliki yawmi alddeeni
001:005 You alone we serve, and You alone we seek for help. Iyyaka naAAbudu wa-iyyaka nastaAAeenu
001:006 Guide us to the straight path. Ihdina alssirata almustaqeema
001:007 The path of those whom You have blessed, not those who have incurred the wrath, nor the misguided. Sirata allatheena anAAamta AAalayhim ghayri almaghdoobi AAalayhim wala alddalleena
