58088Quran (Progressive Muslims Organization) — 85

1-22[edit]

  In the name of God, the Almighty, the Most Merciful. Bismi Allahi alrrahmani alrraheemi
085:001 By the universe laden with towers. Waalssama-i thati alburooji
085:002 And the appointed Day. Waalyawmi almawAAoodi
085:003 And a witness and a witnessed. Washahidin wamashhoodin
085:004 Dead are the people of the canyon. Qutila as-habu alukhdoodi
085:005 The Fire supplied with fuel. Alnnari thati alwaqoodi
085:006 They will be placed in it. Ith hum AAalayha quAAoodun
085:007 And they were witness to what they did to the believers. Wahum AAala ma yafAAaloona bialmu/mineena shuhoodun
085:008 And all they had asked them was to believe in God, the Noble, the Praiseworthy! Wama naqamoo minhum illa an yu/minoo biAllahi alAAazeezi alhameedi
085:009 The One to whom belongs kingship of heavens and Earth, and God is witness over everything. Allathee lahu mulku alssamawati waal-ardi waAllahu AAala kulli shay-in shaheedun
085:010 Those who have put the believing men and women under ordeal, and then did not repent, they will have the retribution of Hell, and they will have the retribution of burning. Inna allatheena fatanoo almu/mineena waalmu/minati thumma lam yatooboo falahum AAathabu jahannama walahum AAathabu alhareeqi
085:011 Those who believe and do good works, they will have gardens with rivers flowing beneath. Such is the great reward. Inna allatheena amanoo waAAamiloo alssalihati lahum jannatun tajree min tahtiha al-anharu thalika alfawzu alkabeeru
085:012 The punishment of your Lord is severe. Inna batsha rabbika lashadeedun
085:013 It is He who initiates and then returns. Innahu huwa yubdi-o wayuAAeedu
085:014 And He is the Forgiver, the Most Kind. Wahuwa alghafooru alwadoodu
085:015 Possessor of the throne, the Glorious. Thoo alAAarshi almajeedi
085:016 Doer of what He wills. FaAAAAalun lima yureedu
085:017 Has news come to you of the soldiers? Hal ataka hadeethu aljunoodi
085:018 Pharaoh and Thamud? FirAAawna wathamooda
085:019 No, those who rejected are in denial. Bali allatheena kafaroo fee taktheebin
085:020 And God after them is encompassing. WaAllahu min wara-ihim muheetun
085:021 No, it is a glorious Quran. Bal huwa qur-anun majeedun
085:022 In a tablet, preserved. Fee lawhin mahfoothin
