1-3[edit]

  In the name of God, the Almighty, the Most Merciful. Bismi Allahi alrrahmani alrraheemi
108:001 We have given you plenty. Inna aAAtaynaka alkawthara
108:002 Therefore, you shall reach out to your Lord, and sacrifice. Fasalli lirabbika wainhar
108:003 Indeed your rival will be the loser. Inna shani-aka huwa al-abtaru
